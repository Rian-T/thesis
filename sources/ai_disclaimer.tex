\medskip

\noindent AI agents let me try a different way of brainstorming. I would talk out loud
about the plan of the thesis, the order of the arguments, and the narrative to
build, while the agent took notes in the codebase and adjusted it as I
spoke.\footnote{The agent sometimes wrote into the plan something slightly
different from what I had said. Once in a while, that small shift was a better
organisation than the one I had proposed.}

\bigskip

\bigskip
\noindent This had two effects. The agent wrote the plan down for me, so I could
just keep talking and focus on the narrative. Second, talking
turned out to be productive and stimulating to me, or at least pleasant. It made problems easy to catch: a weak point \textit{sounds bad} when you say it out
loud.

\medskip

\noindent I first wrote most of the paragraphs of this thesis informally in my
native language, French, in something close to text-message style. AI agents helped me turn
them into academic English, through several rounds of rewriting, mine and the
agent's.
