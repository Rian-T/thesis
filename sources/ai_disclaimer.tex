\noindent\textbf{Brainstorming out loud.}\quad I think by talking: walking the
room, saying the argument as it comes. Speech transcription turns that stream
into a first draft, which the assistant then structures.

\begin{center}

    {Bon\dots\ la partie sur les t\^aches cliniques. Faut montrer que le

\noindent\textbf{Iterative writing.}\quad I draft in French, fast and informal.
The assistant returns an English version; I correct, it revises, until the
sentence says exactly what I mean.

\bigskip
\begin{center}
\resizebox{\textwidth}{!}{%
\begin{tikzpicture}

  % --- b1: me, French draft ---
