A corpus is only useful once a model has learned from it. This part puts the
biomedical corpus to work and asks what makes pretraining on it effective. We first
try the naive approach: continuing the pretraining of an existing French encoder on
biomedical text. We then look past masked language modeling to the training
objective itself, and finally bring structured medical knowledge into what the
model reads.
